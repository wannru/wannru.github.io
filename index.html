            Projects

              • Susun Kata
                Susun Kata is a simple emoji guessing game. You must arrange the words with emojis and match them. If the words correspond with the meaning of emojis, you win the level! This game is not easy to play, not just tap-tap win. You must think of it. If not, you may lose many points or just get one point. Get the score as many as you can! At the end of the game, the score will be inserted into leaderboard (You must be logged into Google Play Service in advance).
              • Ball Gelinding
                Ball Gelinding is a ball race game between two-player connected to internet with an accelerometer as a controller. Your goal is to be the first who reach the finish. Cautious with the path and control your phone carefully which one is your steering!
              • Tank Defense
                Tank Defense is the next game (currently still in progress) that I want to publish on the store both Android and iOS. This game consists of single-player and dual-player (LAN). In the single-player mode, this will be an endless game type. You must destroy enemies as much as you can, obtain, upgrade, evade, shoot, move, and do everything you can to survive and acquire high points as well. In dual player mode, you can play with your friends or brother or anyone, and they become your enemy.
