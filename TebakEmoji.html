Susun Kata is a simple emoji guessing game. You must arrange the words with emojis and match them. If the words correspond with the meaning of emojis, you win the level! This game is not easy to play, not just tap-tap win. You must think of it. If not, you may lose many points or just get one point. Get the score as many as you can! At the end of the game, the score will be inserted into leaderboard. This game only available in Indonesian language.
